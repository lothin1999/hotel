Coastal Routes

Six Legendary
Roads

Each of our six curated routes was personally mapped by our head of moto experiences, Luca Ferretti. Every road is assessed weekly for safety, scenery, and discovery.

GPS-loaded route cards are prepared for each rental. Every route includes rest stops, fuel points, emergency contacts, and hidden viewpoints unknown to public maps.

01
Cliff's Edge Circuit
42km · 2.5 hrs · Intermediate
The signature route. A serpentine coastal road carved into the volcano's southern face, with three unmissable overlooks.
★ Signature
02
Jungle Pass Loop
68km · 4 hrs · Advanced
Dense forest switchbacks through the interior, emerging at the island's highest peak with panoramic views of all shores.
03
Sunrise Bay Dawn Ride
28km · 1.5 hrs · Beginner
The gentlest route, perfectly timed for the island's famous 6am sunrise over the eastern bay. Departs at 5:30am.
04
Village & Vineyard Trail
55km · 3.5 hrs · Intermediate
A cultural journey through three historic villages with a private tasting at Estate Noir winery arranged for all Velour guests.
